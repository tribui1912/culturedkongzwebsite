The Cultured Kongz are here to set the standard for the future of Solana.

In order to accomplish this, our goal is to have most of the project ready pre-mint. We will be giving constant updates on
our progress. It is important to show the community that you can build a project before mint. Too many projects do not
have anything ready until weeks/months post-mint. Our delivery will be the reason why this project will be remembered
in the history of Solana.

The Kongz Collection will be delivering an Arcade, Staking mechanisms, Upgrading, and a doxing feature. Our arcade
will be using the token $KOINZ. You do not need a Kongz NFT to play in our arcade, but you will need a Kongz NFT
if you want to earn our $KOINZ through our staking process.

Our $KOINZ will be our main in game currency, and will be used to upgrade our NFTs.

This is only the start of what we have planned, we have more things to come post mint...
