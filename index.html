The Cultured Kongz aims to establish redefined tokenomics in the Solana
ecosystem. The NFT collection will feature lore and rewards through unique
staking mechanics. These mechanics are driven by utility and longevity.
We want the community to see that it is possible to create longevity in
the Solana ecosystem by producing quality and sustainable tokenomics.
Our NFTs will be useful, valuable, and will give back in many ways.
The team has put together stable and fun elements to incorporate into
the utility of the Cultured Kongz NFTs. The team is here for the long
term; we will be constantly updating and planning new phases of utility
for the NFTs.

Profession
             Professions will be essential to progress your Kongz NFTs.  
          Each profession will be delegated specific tasks, and ONLY that profession
        can complete that specific task.For example, the Engineer profession is
      necessary to build a Spaceship; no other profession can do this (unless you
    have an Apex trait). The current available professions are located in
The academy.
Building
                Building will be one of the activities that your Kongz NFT can perform.
             After earning the Engineer profession and spending some $KOINZ, you
           will be able to send your Kongz NFT to The Hangar. Here, your Kongz will
         be able to start building parts for your spaceship. After gathering all
       the necessary parts, you will be able to put together those parts to
     complete and mint a Spaceship NFT. Only 888 Spaceship NFTs will be minted,
   so make sure to build them as soon as you can! More info about builds in
The hanger .
exploration
              After acquiring a spaceship, you will be able to send your Kongz NFT to
            other star systems and discover planets. Currently, you are required to
          have at least 1 Kongz NFT with the Pilot profession and 1000 $KOINZ token
        to use the spaceship.
